About THE HOTSPOT

The Hotspot is a youth club in Nechells, inner-city Birmingham. It was created to give young people the opportunities and support they deserve.

We help young people grow mentally and physically, building skills for life and creating pathways to brighter futures.

Our work includes Mentoring & Employability (16–19) and Sports & Activities.

Nechells is one of the most deprived areas in the West Midlands, with limited opportunities for social mobility. That is why we exist: to change the story.

Backed by a diverse team of youth practitioners with real-life experience, we provide mentoring, coaching, and support that meets young people where they are and helps them get where they want to be.

Programs

Mentoring (16–19)

Employability and life skills to progress with confidence:

  • Networking and online presence
  • Emotional intelligence and communication
  • Critical thinking and decision-making
  • Skills needed by young men and women today

Physical Activities

Stay active and build discipline through:

  • HAF camps
  • National and international trips
  • Weekly football sessions
  • Archery and wrestling
